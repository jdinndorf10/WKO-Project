Volunteer

Types of Gifts:

Service projects on-site or off-site for teens and adults.

Method of Giving:

Donate your time to help a particular program at WKO.

Benefits to Donor:

Satisfaction of directly helping the children and families served, some volunteer hours may have tax benefits – consult your tax adviser, and work for WKO helps teens and others satisfy community service requirements for school and church.

Outright Gift

Types of Gifts:

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds).

Method of Giving:

Donor transfers assets to Whole Kids Outreach online or by mail.

Benefits to Donor:

Tax deductible to the full extent of the law, gifts of stocks and bonds help a donor avoid capital gains tax, and donors qualifying for tax credit programs are eligible for additional state tax credits.

Employer Matching Gift

Types of Gifts:

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds).

Method of Giving:

Send in a matching gift form from you or your spouse’s employer and WKO will complete and submit it for the contribution. Many companies also match retiree and spouse gifts.

Benefits to Donor:

Tax deduction only for individual’s gift portion, and corporation recognized as donor.

Pledge

Types of Gifts:

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds).

Method of Giving:

Written agreement with WKO with a promise to make several contributions over a short-term period.

Benefits to Donor:

Tax deduction as each contribution on the pledge is made.

Planned Gift

Types of Gifts:

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds).

Method of Giving:

A provision in an estate plan such as a bequest in a will to transfer assets to Whole Kids Outreach.

Benefits to Donor:

Control over distribution of estate, and avoidance of inheritance tax.

Gift-In-Kind

Types of Gifts:

A vast array of items is needed for our many programs. See our most up-to-date wish list and more explanations.

Method of Giving:

Bring or ship items to WKO or request assistance for transporting, and donated items are also needed for the Share A Toy Christmas Store and WKO’s annual auction.

Benefits to Donor:

Tax-deductible to the full extent of the law, and value of gift is determined by donor and financial adviser.

Special Events

Types of Gifts:

Cash, appreciated securities (stocks and bonds).

Method of Giving:

Attend a special event to raise support for WKO and/or be a sponsor of an event. WKO’s major event is an annual dinner auction.

Benefits to Donor:

Tax deductible to the full extent of the law, sponsors are recognized at the event, and have a great time in the company of others who also support the mission of WKO.

Third Party Events

Types of Gifts:

Cash or appreciated securities (stocks and bonds) or gifts-in-kind, dependent on event.

Method of Giving:

Host an event to benefit WKO or have a party and ask for gifts that will be donated to help WKO (such as for the Share a Toy Christmas Store or for baby items).

Benefits to Donor:

Special recognition as a supporter of WKO.
