Whole Kids Outreach’s Summer Day Camp strives to provide a carefully supervised and fun environment for children to build lifelong memories, friendships, and new skills. The wellness-oriented camp includes four one-week session during the summer (typically mid-June through mid-July) for area children ages 4-12. Children may attend all four weeks or selected weeks. Included in the camp activities are daily horse riding and swimming lessons as well as art projects and each week’s theme activities. Camp is a forum for both youth-volunteer activities and employment opportunities for teens and young adults who are hired as counselors and counselors-in-training.
