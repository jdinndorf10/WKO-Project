The Whole Kids Outreach Center is designed as an oasis of hope in the rural Missouri. Whole Kids Outreach has continuously worked to create a safe haven for families at Center. The main building – the Mission House – and 10 acres that make up the “Center” have been owned and operated by WKO since 1999. Also on site, are the Blessing House; a small office and activities building; the stable and riding arena; an activities/storage building; and, the Giving House, which can accommodate up to 24 guests to the WKO site.Center Based Programs
